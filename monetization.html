Website Monetization Restrictions

Problem: Age and Regional Restrictions

Why This Happens
  • Financial compliance laws require identity verification.
  • Age restrictions prevent underage accounts from earning.
  • Regional policies can restrict payments or ad serving.
Step-by-Step Solution
  1. Choose community-driven or educational monetization platforms.
  2. Use local payment methods such as Mobile Money if supported.
  3. Verify age and identity if required by the platform.
  4. Focus on building trust and authority before monetizing.
  5. Maintain accurate records for compliance purposes.
Best Practices
  • Monetization follows value, not traffic.
  • Comply with regional and platform policies.
  • Document your monetization setup clearly for audit purposes.
