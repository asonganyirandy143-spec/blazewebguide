HTTPS & Monetization Guide

Problem: HTTPS Warnings

Browsers warn users even with SSL due to mixed content or wrong certificates.

Solution:
  • Force HTTPS redirects.
  • Check certificate covers both www and root domains.
  • Remove all HTTP content.

Problem: Monetization Restrictions

Ad platforms restrict monetization due to age or regional policies.

Solution:
  • Use community-driven or educational monetization platforms.
  • Integrate local payment systems like Mobile Money.
  • Build trust and authority before monetization.
